This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze and visualize online sales data, providing meaningful business insights into sales performance, customer behavior, and trends.
🚀 Features


📈 Sales Insights Dashboard

Total Sales: 438K+
Total Profit: 37K+
Total Quantity Sold: 5615
Monthly sales trends visualization
Sales distribution by customer name
Payment mode analysis (COD, UPI, Debit Card, etc.)
Detailed order breakdown with profit and city-level information



👤 Customer Insights Dashboard

Total Customers: 336
Average Sales per Customer: 1.30K
Profit analysis across customers
Sales by sub-category (clothing, accessories, electronics, etc.)
Customer distribution across states in India
Top customers by sales contribution



🛠️ Tools & Technologies

Power BI
Data Modeling & DAX Measures
Data Cleaning & Transformation (Power Query)
Interactive Visualizations

📌 Key Insights

Identified top-performing customers and regions
Analyzed sales patterns over time
Evaluated profit contribution by product categories
Compared payment mode usage trends
Enabled data-driven decision making for business growth
